POS Dynamic Combo/POS Combo Creation

Dynamic Combo creation in POS.

Enable Combo/Pack in products and add combo sub products in combo product lines.

Select price option.

Clicking on the combo product will open a popup.Set quantity of combo sub products and click Add button to order the combo. click AddMore button to add more products in the combo.

Clicking on the Add More button will open a new popup. click plus button to add new sub products in combo and click Add button to order the combo

Combo product in order line after order
