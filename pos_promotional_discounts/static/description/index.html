How Do Promotional Discounts Work Effectively in Increasing Conversions?

For maximizing your sales, offering promotional offers & discounts is one of the best techniques.

Promotional Discounts are one of the best ways to sway customers to your Store. About 55% of people first check for a discount before buying.

Now Take advantage of various strategies in Odoo POS Promotional Offers to build a customer base for your store.

Introducing Odoo POS Condition-Based Promotional Discount & Offers

Combine the desired Condition Type with any type of discount to create unique Promotional Offers in Odoo POS for your customers.

Types Of Conditions you can choose from

  • For Every new customer.
  • For every nth order placed on POS.
  • For Every xth order placed in POS.
  • For first x customers.
  • For a Specific Date.

Types of Discount Available for Each Condition

  • Straight percentage Discount.
  • Buy 1 Get one Discount.
  • Total Cart amount Discount.
  • Free Complimentary product Discount.

Use Odoo POS Promotional Discounts to gain new customers & Retain Old Ones.......How?

Combine Promotions types and discounts to cater to every type of customer and occasion to increase your store customer base and conversions.

The offer discount applied is auto-enabled when the set condition is fulfilled in the POS Shop session.

Note: Each offer works independently for each POS Shop and shop session


Introduce Odoo POS First Customer Discount Order

Apply automatic promotion on your store for new customers through Odoo POS.

Bring in more customers with a first-time POS purchase discount.

If enabled the POS promotion is auto-applied to the customer's order.

Odoo POS discounts & Offers On Specific Date

Run season sales and promotions with Odoo POS.

Set the date in the backend and the offer is auto-enabled in Odoo POS on that date.

Give direct discounts on products or give away complimentary free products with purchases.

POS Discount Offer for First X Customers

Increases sales volume by giving discounts and goodies to the first 10, 20, 50, 100, or any number of customers that shop from your store.

POS Promotion for every nth Order

Gives a big discount to lucky customers in your store.

Set special POS Promotional offers and discounts on the specific order number in POS Session.

Facilitates Odoo POS Condition Based Offers & Discounts

  • Create new POS promotions that suit your POS store.
  • You can create offers for special occasions like Christmas, Thanksgiving, etc.
  • Create discounts for various customer types such as the first customers, lucky customers, etc.

Create Unique POS Promotional Offers For Customers

  • Combine various Discount types with different conditions.
  • Create unique purchase offers for customers in Odoo POS.
  • Give direct discounts or free products with POS purchases on your store.

Add POS Promotions On Products Or Product Categories

  • Add the Odoo POS condition-based promotions on various levels.
  • Set promotional offers on different products such as all products, product categories, or selected products.

Create Different POS Condition Based Discount Configurations

  • You can create different Promotion configurations in the backend.
  • Each shop can have its own individual POS Promotion configuration.
  • You can create a new POS promotion configuration for each condition.

See Available Discounts For A Product/Customer In POS

  • The POS user can also see the available offers on the product added to the cart.
  • Apply the available discount to the product with a click.
  • If POS promotions are enabled, the offer gets auto-applied when customers add the product to the cart.

Enable/Disable Promotion From POS Session

  • A manual tab is added to the POS shop to toggle promotions on/off manually.
  • The POS Shop user can enable/disable the POS promotions with a click.

Multi-POS Shop Compatible

  • Each pos shop can have its promotional discount configuration.
  • Create independent promotions and offers for each POS Shop.
  • Each POS promotion works individually for each POS shop.

Show Discount Details On The Odoo POS Receipts

  • The offer/discount type is visible on the Odoo POS receipt.
  • In the Odoo POS, each orderline shows the applied promotional discount.
